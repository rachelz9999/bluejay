FAQs

On what platforms is the app available?
  • For therapists, the app is available on iOS iPad. We also have the web portal available for therapists to use. For patients, the app is available on iOS iPad and iPhone through the Apple App Store, as well as for Androids in Google’s Play Store.
Can I create my own videos?
  • Absolutely! We strive to be as customizeable as we can for therapists and patients! If you would like a step by step walkthrough on how to do this, please contact support@bluejayhealth.com.
Where do I download the app?
  • The app is available cross-platform to both therapists and patients: Therapist iPad, Therapist Web Portal , Patient iOS (iPhone/ iPad), Patient Android.
How much does it cost?
  • The app is free to use for both therapist and patients who register now!
Is it HIPAA compliant?
  • Yes. Here at BlueJay Mobile Health, we take patient confidentiality seriously-- our app is HIPAA compliant.
What's different about this app compared to similar ones?
  • Our app isn't simply an at home exercise program generator; we are a patient engagement tool! We have a HIPAA compliant mobile communication portal and a way to monitor progress and treatment compliance. We empower patients by giving them the right tools to get better, faster.
Who do I contact if I have issues with the app?
  • You can reach us at support@bluejayhealth.com or call us at 925.292.7775.
Who do I contact to see a demo?
  • You can reach us at info@bluejayhealth.com to get a web demo of the application.
How do my patients receive their treatment plan?
  • When you register a patient on your end, the patient will receive an email with a link to download the app. When your patients download the app and register with the same phone number under which you registered them, they will have access to all the videos and notes that you send.
How can I get this implemented in my clinic?
  • It's as simple as downloading the app from the App Store, or using our web portal pt.bluejayhealth.com. You can also contact us at 925.292.7775.
