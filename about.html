About

BlueJay Mobile Health is a mobile health company dedicated to equipping physical therapists, healthcare professionals, and patients with personalized mobile applications to enable effective care. The company was founded by a group of health professionals, software engineers and experienced entrepreneurs from the San Francisco Bay Area.
